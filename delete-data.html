Data Deletion Instructions

If you have used Facebook Login or created an account with Tabor Study, you may request deletion of your personal data at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

Please send an email to support@taborstudy.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Tabor Study account

Once we verify your identity, we will permanently delete your data from our systems within 7 business days.

Automated Deletion Endpoint (Optional)

If you are a developer or using the Facebook Data Deletion Callback, you can also send a DELETE request to: 

https://taborstudy.com/api/delete-user-data

This endpoint will remove all user information associated with the Facebook user ID.

Thank you for using Tabor Study.
